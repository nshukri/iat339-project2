How does it work?

who are we?

Mealbox is an one-stop solution to get healthy and fresh meals delivered to your door. Instead of spending time shopping and cooking, now you can get your meals delivered straight to your door. A meal box that uses fresh and local ingredients to help fuel and energize our customers throughout their day.

Step 1: Select your plan

Choose from our 4 subscription plans, pick the one that suits you best.

Step 2: Order + Delivery

Choose a meal from our menu you would like to have and select when you would like to have it. We will deliver what you ordered in under an hour.

Step 3: Enjoy!

Enjoy your healthy and fresh Mealbox meal within the comfort of your own home or office.
